Enjoy A Comfortable Premium Living Experience At The Most Convenient Address Of Lahore

Park Central is a high-end luxury apartment complex, that provides luxurious living in the most central location of Lahore. It is an urban oasis offering the finest standards of amenities and services. Located in the central yet serene residential neighbourhood of Shah Jamal, it ensures that you are away from noise and pollution yet very close to everything that matters.

Park Central is designed to provide contemporary, elegant and spacious family oriented living spaces that are modern yet cater to our traditional lifestyles. The central location, unmatched amenities & features designed to cater to the well being of its residents, expansive layouts of apartments based on minimalist architecture and sophisticated design, all combine together to provide an unforgettable living experience.

Location

THE MOST CONVINIENT AND PREMIUM ADDRESS

Park Central is located in the most central high-end residential neighborhood of Lahore.

Nestled in the middle / confluence of three signal free corridors – Canal road, Jail Road and Ferozepur Road, Park Central has an unbeatable location that is 10 minutes from everything (schools, hospitals, shopping, fitness centres). Its central location means that any part of Lahore is easily and quickly accessible from one of the 3 signal free corridors making it the most convenient location for you and your family. Spend time on things that matter – not in traffic jams.
